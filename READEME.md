# Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Prediction System

## Business Problem
Predict future revenue generated by a customer to support marketing and retention decisions.

## Dataset
Online Retail II dataset containing transactional data of an e-commerce company.

## Problem Formulation
This is a supervised regression problem.

Given historical transaction data of customers over an observation window,
the goal is to predict the total revenue generated by each customer over a future time window.

## Target Variable
Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) defined as total future revenue per customer.
